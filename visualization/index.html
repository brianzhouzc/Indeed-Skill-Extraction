The graphs are split into two parts: the relationship diagrams and the word clouds.

The Sankey and Chord diagram shows the relationship between different skills and different job titles. It shows how jobs share the same skill sets as well as having it's own unique skill requirements.

The Word Cloud diagrams shows what skills are the most sought-after for each job title. It shows the frequency of each skill appearing in the job postings

Interact with the graph to see more details on the data! Hovering over elements highlights the relationship and occurrences of each skill.
